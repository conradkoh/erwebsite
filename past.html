ER Missions Trip 2015 [8 - 14 December]

12/26/2015

0 Comments

 
The Bethel Expedition Rangers went on a missions trip to Iloilo city from the 8th - 14th of December 2015. It was a great experience, packed with many activities and memories, in the process forging new friendships with many people from the local churches and schools.

This missions trip has been one with many challenges, from the planning of our school ministries, to preaching at churches, and even our outreach camp! But we thank God for providing for all our needs every step of the way, ensuring our safety for the whole duration of the trip. In spite of the challenges we faced, we were able to overcome. 

Bearing in mind our common purpose - to spread God's love to the children and the people of Iloilo, we may think at times that with our money we are in a far greater position to bless them; to meet their needs and to bring our insight and our knowledge to them. Yet through their simple smiles and their genuine love we find that it is us who have been blessed, and reminded of what is truly important in life - Loving God and loving people.

I have also been reminded through disagreements and challenges faced in working with people, that each one of us is essential in a team, and that we must always pour out our love to those around us, unceasingly and unreservedly, because they are as important as the objectives that we are trying to achieve. Each person who has contributed their time and poured out their heart into this missions trip has made a difference, and touched a life through their words and simple acts of kindness, that will not easily be forgotten. 

Thank God for this opportunity to make new friends, meet up and look on the faces of familiar old ones, just a little older - always faithful. Praise be to God!
